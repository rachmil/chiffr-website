How it Works.

Follow these steps to get started with Chiffr:

  1. Download the app and sign up with your email address. You will receive a unique username, such as "strongtrumpet281".
  2. Create a new AES Key or Stream Cipher in the Encryption tab.
  3. In the Encryption tab, select the key you generated and have your contact tap download .
  4. Once the secret key is shared, an encrypted conversation will start automatically, ensuring all messages exchanged are secure.
  5. You can repeat Steps 2 and 3 to generate and share a new key whenever you like. This will not erase your chat history.

Current key exchange methods (e.g., Diffie-Hellman and Elliptical Curve Cryptography) rely on the difficulty of solving mathematical problems to securely establish a shared secret. Quantum computers could intercept and calculate these keys, enabling them to decrypt all communication between parties.

A cipher (SY-fur) is a binary data file shared between contacts. Each message uses a unique portion of the cipher for encryption, and it is never re-used for another message.

Exchanging a cipher or secret key ensures both parties share a secure key for encrypting and decrypting messages. By avoiding online or cloud transmission, the key remains private and fully secure.

Chiffr is a spin on "Chiffre," the French word for "cipher" or "key." It symbolizes the secure key that keeps your messages private.

Our cryptography is built on the One-Time Pad (OTP) method. Each piece of the cipher is used exactly once for each bit of plaintext message. This uniqueness is why OTP is theoretically unbreakable.
